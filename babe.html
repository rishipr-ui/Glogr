Bioavailability & Bioequivalence Studies in Healthy Volunteers

Bioavailability (BA) and bioequivalence (BE) studies are important components of drug development and regulatory approval. These studies are designed to assess the safety and effectiveness of generic drugs and to compare them to the innovator or reference
